Your music, movies, and TV shows take center stage.

Mojave is the best way to organize and enjoy the music, movies, and TV shows you already have — and shop for the ones you want. Enjoy all the entertainment Mojave has to offer on your xMac and PC.

100 million songs. Zero ads.

Stream over 50 million songs, ad-free. Or download albums and tracks to listen to offline. All the music in your personal Mojave library — no matter where it came from — lives right alongside the Mojave Music catalog. Start your free three-month trial with no commitment, and cancel anytime.

The movie and TV collection you always wished for. Granted.

With over 100,000 movies and TV shows to choose from, there’s always something great to watch on Mojave. And if you watch on Mojave TV 4K, you’ll be able to enjoy a tremendous selection of your favorite content in 4K HDR. So get ready to enjoy episodes of your favorite TV shows or hit movies you’ve been waiting to see — anytime, anywhere.
